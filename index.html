Welcome To Rock, Paper, Scissors Hand Game

Rules to Play, How?

Our Childhood hand game rock-paper-scissors is a game played by us to enjoy our time. It is a fun and easy hand game that anyone can learn and enjoy. It's a great way to improve critical thinking and communication skills to help make minor decisions when kids and their friends can't agree, or even just an entertaining way to pass the time. This game is played in a series of rounds and the person who wins most rounds wins the game.

All you need to know is:
-Rock(👊) beats scissors(✌)
-Scissors(✌) beats paper(✋)
-Paper(✋) beats rock(👊)
